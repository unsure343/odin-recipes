Pinto Beans

Description

Note, I borrowed this image from https://addapinch.com/pinto-bean-supper-favorite-southerisms/ until I can make my own batch and change to a much uglier picture.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of dried pinto beans (ideally soaked overnight and then drained)
  • 1 large white onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cups of vegetarian "chicken" broth
  • 3 cups of water
  • Several cloves of garlic (to taste)
  • Cumin (to taste)
  • Oregon (to taste)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (to taste)

Directions

  1. Chop vegetables to small dice size
  2. (Optional) Saute carrot, onion, jalapeno until soft, add garlic and cook another couple minutes
  3. Combine all ingredients in a pressure cooker or crock pot
  4. If beans are not soaked, cook for 50 minutes in pressure cooker on high or at least 10-12 hours in crock pot on high
  5. If beans are soaked, cook for 25 minutes in pressure cooker on high or 8-10 hours in crock pot on high
